About developer

QR code allows smart phone to access your website easily.

Enter or past in the URL, click on Generate when you are done.

It will generate the QR code image, you can decide to download the image by clicking on the download button.

You can also click on Add to data-base, this will save the image to the data-base, you can view them by clicking on the Data-Base button above,you can decide to download, delete or edit it latter. But to do this you have to login if you have account or registerif you don't

The Width and Height will affect when you download it on your device.
